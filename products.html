Products

AWARE

AWARE gives you a real time visibility of how you are consuming energy, broken down by operational processes and assets. Our AWARE product is a powerful analytics platform designed to help organizations gain deep understanding of their energy consumption. Tailored for discrete manufacturing and process industries, and energy-conscious enterprises, our solution transforms raw data into clear knowledge through our intuitive dashboards By integrating data from smart energy meters AWARE provides real-time monitoring, historical trend analysis, and predictive forecasting. With seamless integration into your existing Enterprise IT systems and support for industry standards, AWARE is scalable, secure, and ready for deployment across diverse sectors.

INSIGHT

INSIGHT helps you to understand what is driving your energy usage and wastage and identify areas where there are opportunities to optimize. Operational managers can identify inefficiencies, uncover hidden energy wastage and benchmark performance across sites or assets. With pre-built dashboards and automated reporting, decision-makers gain full understanding of their operations, and opportunities to reduce operational costs and meet sustainability targets with confidence. INSIGHT product also includes advanced features such as anomaly detection, carbon impact visualization, and scenario modeling—enabling decision makers to simulate the benefits of process changes, demand shift scenarios and energy-saving initiatives before committing to implementation. .

ACTION

ACTION provides AI based recommendations on how you can reduce energy usage, carbon footprint and improve overall business performance. Whether your goal is to cut costs, achieve net-zero emissions, or meet regulatory compliance, ACTION provides the clarity and control needed to take decisive action. The recommendations from ACTION creates a template for initiatives based on extensive data collected through our AWARE and INSIGHT model and puts you on the right path to achieving your goals. The initiatives are contextualised by recommended benchmarks for cost, performance, return on investment and larger environmental impacts due to carbon reduction. ACTION delivers everything your decision makers need to be confident about their outcomes and prioritization of investments and delivery.

FINOP

FINOP provides an accurate reconciliation between your energy usage and bills helping you with clear cost accounting and eliminating payable errors. FINOP s an advanced reconciliation algorithm that ensures complete transparency and accuracy in energy billing. Designed for businesses managing multiple sites or high energy consumption, FINOP allows to accurately match actual energy usage and utility invoices, helping organizations detect discrepancies and avoid overcharges. By integrating directly with usage data from smart energy meters and utility providers, FINOP automatically compares usage with billed charges. It validates rates, tariffs, demand charges, and taxes, flagging any mismatches or anomalies for review. FINOP allows finance teams to ensure every energy invoice reflects true usage—no more guesswork, manual audits, or billing surprises. With support for complex billing structures and multi-location management, FINOP is ideal for enterprises seeking control over their energy spending.

G-CREDIT

G-CREDIT enables you to fast forward to your future where you have full control on emissions reporting, compliance and monetizing your carbon reductions. G-CREDIT is designed to simplify carbon emissions tracking, streamline compliance reporting, and enable participation in carbon trading markets. By aggregating data from energy use across Scope 1, 2 and 3 definitions, G-CREDIT delivers real-time emissions monitoring aligned with global standards such as the GHG Protocol, TCFD and regional regulatory frameworks. The product automates emissions calculations across Scopes 1, 2, and 3, reducing the burden of manual reporting and ensuring audit-ready compliance submissions. Beyond compliance, G-CREDIT provides you with the right tools to purchase verified offsets or generate and trade surplus credits through integrated registries and exchanges. G-CREDIT turns complex carbon management into a clear, actionable process—whether your organization is pursuing net-zero commitments, preparing for ESG disclosures, or navigating emissions trading schemes.
